Embed Your Google Calendar

To display your calendar here:
1. Open Google Calendar.
2. Hover over the calendar you want to embed (under "My calendars") and click the three dots → Settings and sharing.
3. Scroll down to “Access permissions” and check “Make available to public”.
4. Scroll to “Integrate calendar” → Copy the Embed code URL (the src in the iframe).
5. Paste that URL below and click “Load Calendar”.
